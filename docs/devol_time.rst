Devolatilization time
=====================

Correlation for 95% devolatilization time for wood particles. Based on
experiments with beech wood cyclinders in a fluidized sand bed.

.. math:: t_v = 0.8\,e^{1525 / T_r} d^{1.2}

Nomenclature
------------

| :math:`t_v` - Time to 95% devolatilization (s)
| :math:`T_r` - Temperature of fluidized bed [K]
| :math:`d` - Diameter of wood particle [mm]
