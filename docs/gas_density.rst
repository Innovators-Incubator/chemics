Gas density
===========

The density of a gas can be calculated using its molecular weight, pressure, and
temperature as shown below:

.. math:: \rho_{gas} = \frac{P \cdot MW}{R \cdot T}

Nomenclature
------------

| :math:`\rho_{gas}` - Density of gas (kg/m³)
| :math:`P` - Pressure of the gas (Pa)
| :math:`MW` - Molecular weight of the gas (g/mol)
| :math:`R` - Gas constant as 8.3145 [(m³ Pa)/(K mol)]
| :math:`T` - Temperature of the gas (K)
